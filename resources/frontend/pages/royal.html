Royal

The Royal Hall is a 110 sqm room with panoramic windows overlooking the sunny side. There are several locations in the hall:
● A powdery wardrobe perfect for bachelorette parties, bridesmaid gatherings and boudoir shoots.
● There is a real bath near the window framed by an arch.
● Area with a bed in the dark part of the hall.
● Library. A dark wood bookcase, classic bound books and an antique red armchair with a gold frame, and other armchairs perfectly played with the fireplace and library.
● Fireplace area. Also in dark wood with a large mirror in a gold baguette. Decorated with flowers, candles and a Persian carpet.

In the hall, you can shoot with both natural and studio light. The price includes professional studio equipment of the world manufacturer "Bowens".

NOTE!
1. The start of the services is considered to be the booked time in the studio hall, and not the actual start time of shooting.
2. If the Client is late, the booked time will not be transferred. If the Client is more than 30 minutes late and has not warned about it, it is considered that the Client did not appear, and the cost of the reservation is not refunded.
3. The end of the provision of services is the time when the Client handed over the studio room to the studio administrator, and not the time when the shooting ends.
4. In case of early completion of the shooting, the booked time is paid in full.
