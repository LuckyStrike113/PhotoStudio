Plaza

The Plaza Hall is a spacious, bright hall with windows facing the sunny side, a mixture of palace and casual style.
● area 70 sq.m.
● large panoramic window in the floor measuring 3m x 6m
● made in a classic style: antique bas-reliefs, golden crystal chandelier
● of furniture presented: gold sofa and ottoman, graphite and blue armchair, gray sofa, stylish tables, room devices, mobile wall "Love" decorated with floral flowers
● real fireplace (ignition possible with advance booking)
In the hall, you can shoot with natural and professional studio lighting. The price includes professional studio equipment of the world manufacturer "Bowens".
