Light

Hall "Light" is divided into 2 zones - a zone with a kitchen in light beige tones and a zone with a bed in gray and blue tones.

● area 75 sq.m.
● large windows along the entire length of the hall, dark floor
● high ceiling 5m
● in the hall there are: a bed, an armchair, tables, kitchen furniture with a refrigerator, various decor.

In the hall, you can shoot with natural and professional studio lighting. The price includes professional studio equipment of the world manufacturer "Bowens".

NOTE!
1. The start of the services is considered to be the booked time in the studio hall, and not the actual start time of shooting.
2. If the Client is late, the booked time will not be transferred. If the Client is more than 30 minutes late and has not warned about it, it is considered that the Client did not appear, and the cost of the reservation is not refunded.
3. The end of the provision of services is the time when the Client handed over the studio room to the studio administrator, and not the time when the shooting ends.
4. In case of early completion of the shooting, the booked time is paid in full.
